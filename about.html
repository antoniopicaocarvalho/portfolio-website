About

I am an ambitious, empathetic, interested, creative 24-years-old Portuguese Product Designer. Also, a team player seeking ways to combine my engineering skills with my creative vein. I consider myself a fast learner, and I am highly motivated to continue developing my skills.

My Master’s degree was in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Instituto Superior Técnico, the top engineering university in Portugal. My dissertation focused on creating an AR application, which primarily focused on developing an ecosystem for hospitalized patients.

Regarding my interests, I am very competitive and I love practicing sports, as I have been doing it all kinds all my life. The main ones were trampoline gymnastics and rugby. The first one I practiced at Sporting Clube de Portugal since I remember until my 18 and my greatest achievements were to compete twice in the WAGC (World Ages Group Competition) in Daytona Beach, Florida, United States 2014 and Odense, Denmark 2015. I started playing rugby when I finished my journey in trampoline gymnastics and I currently continue in Clube Rugby São Miguel.
