Summary

inovAR is a Mobile Augmented Reality application (MAR) which primarily focuses on developing an ecosystem for hospitalized patients. It aims to improve the patient’s well-being through self-assessments to facilitate his monitoring, and alleviate the fact of being hospitalized in isolation by exploiting AR technology.

This application was developed for my thesis "How Augmented Reality can help surgeons and patients" in 2021.
